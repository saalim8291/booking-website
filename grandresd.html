Grand residency

Grand residency is located at lively area amid shops and restaurants, which is 1 km from Bandra railway station, 2 km from the popular Bandstand Promenade and 6 km from the grand Siddhivinayak Temple. Featuring balconies and wood floors, the unfussy rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, safes and minibars, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Studios add kitchenettes, while 1- and 2-bedroom apartments provide living rooms. Room service is available 24/7.
